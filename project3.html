Illustrations for Aldi Suisse

As part of my time working in Switzerland, I was assigned the role of creating these cup wrap illustrations for Aldi.

Aldi commissions these illustrations annually for their Christmas advent calendar event. The cups were printed, distributed to every Aldi store in Switzerland and given to shoppers who spent over 50 CHF during the 17th - 20th December 2020.
