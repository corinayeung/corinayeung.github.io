bambino's

MunchStars is an app that promotes healthy eating amongst young children and bambino's is a sub-brand I designed for MunchStars targeted towards parents and guardians of children ages 4 - 12 months.

The identity of the brand was designed to communicate organic and clean blends of fruit and vegetables suitable for weaning and that the product is the best for baby.
