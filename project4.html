Good Change

Good Change is a social app that is designed to encourage young people to continue supporting charities and causes they care about to keep driving the movement of social change.

The brief was set by The Bang, a local digital design agency in Newcastle, for one of my units in my second year of uni. My tutor suggested that a couple of my classmates and I worked on this brief collaboratively and so we did. There were 3 of us so we had lots of time to work on the project and we took individual responsibility in ensuring that we were achieving the targets that we had set ourselves for the end of each session.
